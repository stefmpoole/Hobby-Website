Do you have a strong interest in interior design? For some, it is a hobby, while for others, it is their chosen career. I personally pursue it as a hobby-it is a passion that I am lucky enough to share with my daughter (who also delights in a good "room makeover"). My goal in designing (or sometimes re-designing) is to construct an aesthetically pleasing environment that also conveys a sense of comfort. I am an individual who is perpetually rearranging furniture, capturing screenshots for inspiration, binge-watching home improvement channels, and forever adding to my idea boards on Pinterest.
